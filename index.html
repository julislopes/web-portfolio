About Me

    I’m a Bachelor of Information Technology graduate from Southern Cross University with a diverse professional background spanning healthcare, hospitality, customer service, operations, and technology. Currently, I am further strengthening my analytical and technical capabilities through a Data Analytics Bootcamp, with a strong focus on data, systems, and business processes.

    Before transitioning into IT, I worked as a pharmacist in Brazil, where I developed a high level of attention to detail, problem-solving ability, and experience working in fast-paced, highly regulated environments. Since moving to Australia in 2017, I have built extensive experience across customer-facing and operational roles, adapting quickly to new industries, systems, and challenges while developing strong communication and organisational skills.

    Alongside my professional experience, I have gained hands-on exposure to technical support and system-related tasks, including troubleshooting, operational support, system setup, and assisting users with technical issues. These experiences strengthened my ability to bridge the gap between technical and non-technical teams while maintaining a strong customer-focused approach.

    My journey from healthcare into technology has given me a unique combination of analytical thinking, adaptability, resilience, and people skills. I thrive in dynamic environments, enjoy learning new technologies, and am passionate about solving problems and improving processes through data and technology.
