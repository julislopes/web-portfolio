About Me

    My name is Juliana, and I am eager to embark on my career in the technology sector. Recently, I obtained my Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) from Southern Cross University in Perth, graduating at the end of 2023.


    Before 2017, my career was rooted in healthcare, where I worked as a pharmacist in Brazil. The decision to move to Australia marked a significant turning point in my life. Living abroad has been a daily challenge, one that I am proud to say I have successfully navigated. This experience has taught me resilience and adaptability, qualities that are essential in the ever-evolving field of technology.


    Over the past seven years, I have ventured into diverse areas I had never imagined working in. This journey has strengthened and matured me, equipping me to face any obstacles that come my way. Recognizing the need for a career change and a fresh start, I boldly decided in 2021 to pursue a BIT at Southern Cross University in Perth. This transition from healthcare to technology has been my most significant challenge to date, and I have embraced it with enthusiasm and dedication.

    As I complete my BIT, I am eagerly seeking opportunities to embark on this new career path. I am particularly interested in IT Consultation, Business, Data, or Testing Analysis, but open to other fields.
